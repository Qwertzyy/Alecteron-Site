Greek Religion

The Greek Gods

There are many greek gods such as Zeus, Athena, Hera, Aphrodite, etc. The god of all gods is Zeus, the god of the sky and thunder.

The Olympic Games

The Olympic Games is like a religeous festival that is preformed to honor the greek gods. These olympic games were to be preformed naked/nude. These games include things like foot races, javelin throwing, the pentathlon, etc.

The Great Conquests Of Alexander The Great

When And Where Was Alexander The Great Born?

Alexander The Great was born in Pella. Pella is the ancient capital of Macedonia. He was born in 356 BC.

Who are Alexander's Parents And What Is Their Current Status?

Alexander's father was "King Phillip II" and his mother was "Olympias". His mother was born in 375 BC and his father was born in 382 BC.

How old was Alexander first obtained the unruly horse, that would lead him into countless battles? What was his horse's name?

Alexander The Great was 12 years when he had tamed the unruly horse. The horse devoured all who tried to ride it. The name of the unruly horse was Bucephalus.

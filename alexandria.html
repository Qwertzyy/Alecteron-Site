Alexandria

Alexandria is the capital of Egypt and it was formed by Alexander The Great. Alexandria is also one of Egypts largest cities.

Alexandria's Accomplishments

Alexandria was very well known for it's many accomplishments such as the Lighthouse Of Alexandria, The Grand Library, and The Seven Wonders Of The Ancient World.

The Size Of The City

The city-state is 25 miles in area(that is 40 kilometers) and it goes along a limestone ridge that is 2 miles in area (3.2 kilometers).
